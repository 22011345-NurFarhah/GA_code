    INTEREST RATE LINKED


    What is it ?


    Investments that uses interest rate linked have a greater likelihood of seeing
    higher returns from time to time and depend their returns on changes in the
    rate of interest.

    When is it appropriate to apply for the product ?


    Investors who want market exposure but aren't willing to put their capital
    at risk are excellent candidates.

    These investors may also be people that are attempting to reduce total
    portfolio risk and want to invest while avoiding potential inflation to
    occur.


    Potential Benefits

    Wide range of currency
    to invest from!

    When interest rate grow, foreign investments
    are more likely to be attracted, and there is a
    greater demand for specific currencies to
    purchase goods.
