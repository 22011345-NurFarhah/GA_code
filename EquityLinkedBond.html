    EQUITY - LINKED BOND


    What is it ?


    Equity - Linked Notes are investments that combines fixed income
    investment with additional rewards based on the success of the equity
    market's performance.

    When is it appropriate to apply for the product ?


    Investors who want to protect their financial assets while seeking a potential
    profit compared to traditional bonds might consider this product.


    Investors might take advantage of lower prices or greater rewards by
    purchasing shares from a certain company.

    Potential Benefits

    Protect their financial assets

    To guarantee that the investor would receive the
    initial amount deposited. This aggrement includes
    zero coupon bonds that are paid monthly.

    Flexible

    The investor can select their prefered shares,
    The quantity of shares, and other selections.

    Risks

    Credit Risk

    Due to the product's issuing as an
    unsecured debt obligation of a company due to company's
    trustworthiness. Investors will lose the majority of their
    investment if the issuer fails to do their part.

    Market Risk

    There will be a circumstances where there is a
    change in underlying assets.
