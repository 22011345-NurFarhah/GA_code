    CREDIT-LINKED NOTE


    What is it ?


    Credit - Linked Notes are investments that allow the
    investment with additional rewards based on the success of the equity
    market's performance.

    When is it appropriate to apply for the product ?


    Investors should apply for the product if they are prepared to take a
    risk. This is due to the potential that the investor
    won't be able to profit from the product's sale.

    Potential Benefits

    Protect Investors

    To protect investors against possibility of
    specific credit disaster that could result in
    financial loss.

    Risks

    There may be times where there might
    also be no returns to investors.

    Returns are not consistent due to change
    in market value.

    Investors might need to continuously monitor
    the likelihood of a credit event so they will
    know when to steer clear.
